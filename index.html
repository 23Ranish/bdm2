Our Story

Born From a Passion for Perfection

Black Diamond Motors was founded on a singular belief: that the act of driving should be an experience, not merely a necessity. Our founders, seasoned automotive enthusiasts, saw a void in the market — a place where discerning clients could access world-class vehicles without compromise.


Since our establishment in 2024, we have grown into one of Canada's most respected premium rental car, maintaining an unwavering commitment to quality, discretion, and exceptional service. Our team of dedicated professionals treats every reservation with the same care and attention reserved for the finest luxury purchases.
